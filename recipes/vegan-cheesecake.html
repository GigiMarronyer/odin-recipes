Vegan Cheesecake

Description

Enjoy a plant-based pud using vegan 'cheese' and coconut cream. This no-bake dessert is full of rich vanilla flavour and works well with seasonal fruit.

Ingredients

  • 100g coconut oil, melted, plus extra for the tin
  • 300g digestive vegan biscuits
  • 800g vegan soft cheese
  • 175g soft light brown sugar
  • 175g soft light brown sugar
  • 100g of vegan vanilla yogurt
  • 1 tsp vanilla paste
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 40g coconut cream

Steps

  1. Oil the sides and line the bottom of a 23cm loose-bottom tin. Whizz the biscuits in a food processor until you have a fine crumb, then mix with the 100g melted coconut oil. Press into the prepared tin using the back of a spoon, then chill in the fridge while you make the filling.
  2. Put the vegan soft cheese into a large bowl and use an electric hand mixer to whisk with the sugar until smooth. Combine the yogurt alternative with the vanilla paste, coconut cream and cornflour in a small bowl. Pour this into the vegan cheese mix and whisk again until smooth.
  3. Spread the mix over the base and use a spatula to smooth. Chill in the fridge for 6 hrs until set. Will keep in the fridge for up to three days.
  4. Run a butter knife under the hot tap, then use this to loosen the cheesecake from the sides of the tin. Slice to serve alongside your favourite berries or other seasonal fruit.
